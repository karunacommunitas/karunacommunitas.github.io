Preparation and Integration Practioners

This directory brings together practitioners within the Karuna Communitas network who offer preparation, integration, and other forms of compassionate support.

Please note practitioners in this directory work in different ways and offer different forms of support. We encourage you to read each profile carefully and contact practitioners directly to discuss fit, availability, and scope of practice.

Practitioners listed here should not be contacted regarding the provision, facilitation, or guidance around psychedelic substances.
